# 🏘️ Village Roads — Road Builder & Resource Management Game

A turn-based 3D road builder and resource management game built in Unity, focused on the challenges faced by rural Bulgarian villages — lack of infrastructure, healthcare, and basic resources.

---

## 🎮 Concept

You are tasked with connecting isolated villages by building roads and managing resources under time and energy constraints. Every decision matters: the map is different every run, so no two playthroughs are the same.

The game draws attention to real hardships in rural communities and gives players the opportunity to learn about — and support — real-world foundations that address these issues.

---

## 🗺️ Gameplay

### World Generation
- Procedurally generated 3D square tile grid — unique every game
- Tile types include: mountains, roads, crossroads, trees, farms, houses, mines, factories, rivers, fields, and more

### Turn-Based System
- Each action costs **turns**
- Each action also consumes the relevant resource from your stockpile
- At the start of each day you receive a set amount of wood, stone, and any other resources you've unlocked

### Main Quests
- Time-limited objectives, e.g.:
  > *"Build a road to the next village within 20 days(turns)."*
- Quests reflect real infrastructure challenges in rural areas

### Side Quests & NPCs
- NPCs scattered across the map offer optional side quests
- Side quests cost energy or days, but reward you with ongoing bonuses
  > *Example: Help a villager → they bring you +1 wood every day*
- Introduces meaningful **decision making**: is this side quest worth the cost right now?

### Resource Management
- Balance your stockpile carefully — spend too much on side quests and your main quest may fail
- The optimal strategy changes every run based on the procedurally generated map

---

## 🌱 Real-World Impact

At the end of each game — and accessible at any time from the menu — a screen displays links and descriptions of **real foundations and organizations** that support rural communities in Bulgaria.

The goal is to raise awareness and give players a direct path to take action beyond the game.
